# Story Themes

Theme is what a story is *about*, beyond its plot.

- Don't state the theme explicitly
- Let contrasting characters embody opposing sides
- Theme should emerge through conflict resolution

See [[Storytelling Fundamentals]] · [[Narrative Arcs]] · [[Creative Hub]].
