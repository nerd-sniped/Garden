# Writing Process

The writing process has stages:

1. **Prewriting** — brainstorm, outline
2. **Drafting** — capture ideas without censoring
3. **Revising** — restructure and expand
4. **Editing** — clarity, grammar, flow
5. **Publishing** — share with the world
