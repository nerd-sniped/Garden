# Epistemology

Epistemology is the branch of philosophy concerned with **knowledge**: its nature, sources, scope, and limits.

- What can we know?
- How do we know what we know?
- What is justified belief?

See [[Rationalism]] · [[Empiricism]] · [[Critical Thinking]] · [[Philosophy Hub]].
