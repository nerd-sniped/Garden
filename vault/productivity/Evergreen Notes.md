# Evergreen Notes

Evergreen notes are written to accumulate and evolve over time.

- Atomic: one concept per note
- Concept-oriented (not source-based)
- Densely linked
- Written for long-term future self

See [[Zettelkasten Method]] · [[Progressive Summarisation]] · [[Productivity Hub]].
