# Callouts Guide

Callouts render styled boxes. Use `> [!type]` syntax.

> [!note]
> A default note callout.

> [!tip]
> A helpful tip.

> [!warning]
> A warning box.

> [!danger]
> Danger or error callout.

> [!info]
> Informational callout.

> [!success]
> Success or check callout.

> [!question]
> A question or FAQ item.

> [!example]
> An example block.

> [!quote]
> A blockquote callout.

> [!abstract]
> An abstract or summary.
